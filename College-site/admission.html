Admission

Admissions only through JEE Mains Councelling

Admission to IIIT Manipur will be purely on the basis of the All-India JEE (Main) examination administered by CBSE and the seat allocation will be through Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA 2017). Candidates are required to qualify through JEE (Main) 2017 and students aspiring to join IIIT Manipur have to apply through JoSAA 2017.
